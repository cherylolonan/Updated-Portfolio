About Me


I live simply. If I can laugh heartily, share delicious meals with loved ones, and go on adventures, doing so with kindness, that is happiness for me.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a Biology degree from SDSU and studied medicine at UC Irvine School of Medicine. My professional background has afforded me experience in the biotech industry, clinic and hospital settings, and now the pharmaceutical industry.

A life-long learner, I am currently studying web development at UCSD Extension, with the hopes of improving the technology that promotes healthcare.

Outside of work, most of my free time is spent with friends, family, and my dog - enjoying movies/Netflix, escape rooms, game nights, traveling, and eating (my love for food is STRONG).
