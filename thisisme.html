About Me

I live simply. If I can laugh heartily, share delicious meals with loved ones, go on adventures around the world, and live in kindness, that is happiness for me.


I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a Biology degree from SDSU and then studied medicine at UC Irvine School of Medicine. My professional background has afforded me experience in the biotech industry, direct patient care/healthcare, and currently pharmaceuticals.


I seemingly have touched each aspect of the healthcare system and have seen firsthand how vital innovative technology is to patient care. I therefore became a full stack web developer so I can develop this technology further to improve the patient experience.


When not chasing these aspirations, I spend my free time with friends, family, and my dog - enjoying movies/Netflix, escape rooms, game nights, traveling, and all of the eating.
